Services & Products

We maintain a vast warehouse of electronic parts and components, continuously expanding to meet your evolving needs. To achieve this, we acquire excess stock and obsolete components that are not readily available elsewhere. Furthermore, we are pleased to facilitate the special procurement of components that are not accessible through traditional delivery channels.

GM Global is offering a wide range of electronic components, each designed to deliver optimal performance and reliability.

  • Discrete Semiconductors

    These components are designed for a specific application and operate as single units. They include transistors, diodes, and thyristors, among others.

  • Integrated Processors and Controllers

    These components are designed to perform a specific function, such as controlling a motor or processing data. They include microcontrollers, digital signal processors, and field-programmable gate arrays (FPGAs), among others.

  • Integrated circuits

    These components combine multiple electronic components into a single chip, enabling efficient and cost-effective operation. They include operational amplifiers, voltage regulators, and logic gates, among others.

  • LED lights

    Our selection of LED lights offers efficient and long-lasting lighting solutions for a range of applications. They include standard bulbs, strip lights, and modules, among others.

  • HF Semiconductors

    These high-frequency semiconductors are designed for use in radio frequency (RF) applications. They include amplifiers, oscillators, and transceivers, among others.

  • Memory ICs

    Our memory ICs are used to store and retrieve digital information. They include ROM, RAM, and flash memory, among others.

  • Sensors

    Our range of sensors includes components that detect and measure physical quantities such as temperature, pressure, and light. They include accelerometers, gyroscopes, and temperature sensors, among others.

  • Optoelectronics

    Our optoelectronics components combine electronics and optics to enable communication and sensing through light. They include photodiodes, LEDs, and fiber optic transceivers, among others.
